About me

Hi, I'm Mark Jehnsen Bumanglag

I am an aspiring web developer with a growing passion for creating functional websites and apps for people in Hawai'i. I just recently completed the Web Development Fundamentals course where I learned HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and a little bit of JavaScript. I will continue to learn more about web development through the NuCamp Full-Stack course. Currently, I am working to expand my skills through working on a couple of personal projects to apply what I learned by building responsite websites and aestethically pleasing pages.

Being able to help out smaller communities and organizations in Hawai'i through web development motivates me to keep improving my skills. I want to use my skills to help these communities reach a wider audience and effectively fullfill their goals.
